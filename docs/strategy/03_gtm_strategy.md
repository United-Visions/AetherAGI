```
- Next 30 min: Live demo (we show AetherMind doing it)
- Last 15 min: Q&A + next steps

Customization:
- Use their data (if possible)
- Show their industry vertical
- Address their objections preemptively

Outcome: Pilot or no-go
```

**Step 3: Pilot (Month 1-3)**
```
Goal: Prove value in production environment

Setup:
- 5-10 users (champions)

- Success metrics agreed upfront
- Weekly check-ins

- Task completion time (50% reduction target)

Outcome: Expansion or churn
```
