## üö® Brand Don'ts (Red Flags)

### Never Do These:

1. **‚ùå Hype without substance**
   - "Revolutionary AI" ‚Üê Every AI company says this
   - Instead: Show benchmarks, cite papers

2. **‚ùå Vague claims**
   - "Significantly better" ‚Üê How much?
   - Instead: "30% more accurate" (specific number)

3. **‚ùå Anthropomorphization**
   - "AetherMind thinks like a human"
   - Instead: "AetherMind uses active inference loops"

4. **‚ùå Fear-mongering**
   - "Don't get left behind by AI"
   - Instead: "Save 95% on AI costs"

5. **‚ùå Fake scarcity**
   - "Limited spots available!"
   - Instead: Open source = unlimited access

6. **‚ùå Fake testimonials**
   - Stock photo + generic quote
   - Instead: Real users, real companies, real metrics

7. **‚ùå Over-promising**
   - "Replaces your entire team"
   - Instead: "Handles research, you make decisions"

---

## ‚úÖ Brand Dos (Green Lights)

### Always Do These:

1. **‚úÖ Show benchmarks**
   - Back every claim with data
   - Link to reproducible tests

2. **‚úÖ Be specific**
   - "500K legal cases" not "lots of data"
   - "94% accuracy" not "highly accurate"

3. **‚úÖ Acknowledge limitations**
   - "Day 1: 30% performance"
   - Builds trust

4. **‚úÖ Compare honestly**
   - "GPT-4 is better at general knowledge"
   - "We're better at specialized domains"

5. **‚úÖ Open source credibility**
   - "70% of code is public"
   - "Audit our safety layer"

6. **‚úÖ Technical depth**
   - Don't dumb down for everyone
   - Technical audience appreciates detail

7. **‚úÖ Evolution narrative**
   - "Gets better over time"
   - "Year 1 vs Year 3 comparison"
